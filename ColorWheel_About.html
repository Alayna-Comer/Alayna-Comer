About Us

ColorWheel Candy shop is not just an ordinary candy store; it is a haven for all sugar enthusiasts. We take our candy factory very seriously, and we pride ourselves on offering the most satisfying customer service, day or night.

The story of how ColorWheel Candy Shop came to be is one of triumph and success. It all started in a Web development class. A website was to be made as a final project, and thus, the idea to create a candy wonderland was born. It was hard work making the website at first, but the inevitable success of ColorWheel Candy Shop has shone through!
